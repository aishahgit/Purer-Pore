Actives

Retinoids (Retinol, Retin-A, Retinyl Palmitate)

  • Renews skin by accelerating cell turnover
  • Reduces wrinkles and fine lines
  • Improves skin texture and tone

Hyaluronic Acid

  • Hydrates skin by attracting moisture
  • Improves skin texture and suppleness
  • Supports skin healing and regeneration

Alpha Hydroxy Acids (AHAs - Glycolic Acid, Lactic Acid)

  • Exfoliates to remove dead skin cells
  • Improves skin texture and tone
  • Helps with hyperpigmentation and fine lines

Beta Hydroxy Acid (BHA - Salicylic Acid)

  • Unclogs pores and reduces acne
  • Exfoliates to remove dead skin cells
  • Reduces inflammation and redness

Vitamin C (L-Ascorbic Acid)

  • Brightens skin by fading dark spots
  • Protects from free radicals and UV damage
  • Boosts collagen production

Niacinamide (Vitamin B3)

  • Reduces redness and inflammation
  • Minimizes pore appearance
  • Improves uneven skin tone

Peptides

  • Stimulate collagen productio
  • Minimize fine lines and wrinkles
  • Support skin firmness and elasticity

Ceramides

  • Retains skin moisture
  • Strengthens the skin barrier
  • Improves skin texture and hydration

Squalane/Squalene

  • Moisturizes and hydrates the skin
  • Improves skin elasticity
  • Helps in skin repair and regeneration

Tea Tree Oil

  • Acts as an antibacterial agent
  • Reduces inflammation and acne
  • Soothes irritated skin

Green Tea Extract

  • Provides antioxidant protection
  • Soothes and calms the skin
  • Reduces inflammation and redness

Centella Asiatica (Cica)

  • Calms inflammation and irritation
  • Supports wound healing
  • Strengthens the skin barrier
